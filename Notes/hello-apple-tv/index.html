Hello Apple TV

Sep 8th, 2010

I once dreamed of having a small box in the living room to hold all our movies and music.

The Apple TV that was released four years ago would have given that to me but I didn’t buy it.

At first it seemed a little expensive. Later it seemed like an update would be just around the corner. I didn’t want to be the guy who bought an iPod mini the day before the iPod nano was released.

Last week the long awaited update finally came.

And gosh!

The new Apple TV is so entirely the opposite of what I want that I immediately went out and bought one of the old ones.

The new one isn’t bad. It may well be exactly what Apple should do at this point. But here in Japan we don’t have Netflix or any movies for sale or rent on Tunes at all. The new Apple TV isn’t even available here and the old one has been discontinued. It’s no longer available online but should still be in Apple Stores for a little while.

I’ve only had it for a little while but I think it’s great. I’m still messing about with Handbrake and ATVflash but I think the day that I won’t have to nag the kids (or myself) to put the DVDs away is just around the corner.

The Apple TV is dead!

Long live the Apple TV!
