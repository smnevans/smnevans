An Unhappy Meal

Dec 8th, 2010

I used to have a terrible, terrible diet.

McDonalds was my default place for a quick, filling(!) lunch and I always ordered the same thing: a Big Mac, a Double Cheeseburger, Medium Fries, and a cup of coffee. All that weighs in at just under 1,500 calories and I used to polish it off in five, maybe ten minutes.

Jeez Louise!

This is what it looked like:

Yeah, I’m shaking my head too. The scariest thing was that I would eat this without thinking twice.

Over, and over, and over again.

It’s been quite a while since I had this particular culinary combination and I found it really hard to get through. I didn’t look up the calorie info until I’d finished making the video. If I’d known beforehand how bad it was, I don’t think I’d have made this at all.

Guess who’s having broccoli for dinner.
