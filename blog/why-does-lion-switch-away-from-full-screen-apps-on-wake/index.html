Why Does Lion Switch Away From Full-screen Apps on Wake?

Aug 10th, 2011

What’s going on here?

When I put my MacBook to sleep, Safari (in full-screen) is the active application. When I wake it up it immediately switches to the Finder or some other non-full-screen app.

The same thing happens with every other full-screen app.

I’ve asked the kids, my wife, and the cat and none of us can come up with a reason why that should happen.

Bug? Feature? Just me?

— Update: August 12—

I learned today that switching on wake doesn’t only happen with full-screen apps. It happens all the time. That the internet is not aflame with complaints about this (I can find none) makes me suspect that the problem is local. But I installed Lion on top of a pristine Snow Leopard. What more can I do?
